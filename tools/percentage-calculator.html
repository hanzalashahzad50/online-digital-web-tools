Quick Calculations
Percentage Formulas
Basic Percentage:
Result = Value × (Percentage ÷ 100)
Percentage Change:
Change% = ((New - Old) ÷ Old) × 100
Discount Amount:
Discount = Original × (Discount% ÷ 100)
Markup Amount:
Markup = Cost × (Markup% ÷ 100)

Quick Tips
  • To find 10%, divide by 10
  • To find 5%, divide by 20

  • To find 50%, divide by 2
Percentage Examples
Real-World Examples:
  • Sales Tax: 8.5% on $100 = $8.50
  • Tip: 15% on $50 = $7.50
  • Discount: 20% off $80 = $16 savings
  • Interest: 5% on $1000 = $50 per year
  • Commission: 10% on $500 sale = $50
Business Applications:
  • Profit Margin: (Profit ÷ Revenue) × 100
  • Growth Rate: ((New - Old) ÷ Old) × 100
  • Market Share: (Company Sales ÷ Total Market) × 100
  • Conversion Rate: (Conversions ÷ Visitors) × 100
  • Return on Investment: ((Gain - Cost) ÷ Cost) × 100
