About Energy Units
SI Units:
  • Joule (J): Base unit of energy
  • Kilojoule (kJ): 1,000 joules
  • Megajoule (MJ): 1,000,000 joules
  • Gigajoule (GJ): 1,000,000,000 joules
Electrical Units:
  • Watt-hour (Wh): Energy from 1 watt for 1 hour
  • Kilowatt-hour (kWh): 1,000 watt-hours
  • Megawatt-hour (MWh): 1,000,000 watt-hours
Other Units:
  • Calorie (cal): Energy to heat 1g water by 1°C
  • Kilocalorie (kcal): 1,000 calories (food calorie)
  • British Thermal Unit (BTU): Energy to heat 1lb water by 1°F
  • Electronvolt (eV): Energy gained by electron in 1V potential
  • Foot-pound (ft⋅lb): Energy from 1lb force over 1ft
Usage by Context:
  • Physics: Joules, electronvolts
  • Nutrition: Calories, kilocalories
  • Electricity: Watt-hours, kilowatt-hours
  • Engineering: BTU, foot-pounds
