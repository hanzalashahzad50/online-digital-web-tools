About Length Units
Metric System:
  • Millimeter (mm): 1/1000 of a meter
  • Centimeter (cm): 1/100 of a meter
  • Meter (m): Base unit of length
  • Kilometer (km): 1000 meters
Imperial System:
  • Inch (in): 1/12 of a foot
  • Foot (ft): 12 inches
  • Yard (yd): 3 feet
  • Mile (mi): 5,280 feet
Specialized Units:
  • Nautical Mile: Used in aviation and maritime
  • Light Year: Distance light travels in one year
  • Astronomical Unit: Average distance from Earth to Sun
  • Parsec: Unit used in astronomy
Usage by Country:
  • Metric: Most countries worldwide
  • Imperial: United States, Myanmar, Liberia
  • Mixed: United Kingdom, Canada
