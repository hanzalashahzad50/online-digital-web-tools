Conversion Formulas
Celsius to Fahrenheit:
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
Fahrenheit to Celsius:
°C = (°F - 32) × 5/9
Celsius to Kelvin:
K = °C + 273.15
Kelvin to Celsius:
°C = K - 273.15
Fahrenheit to Kelvin:
K = (°F - 32) × 5/9 + 273.15
Kelvin to Fahrenheit:
°F = (K - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32
Temperature Facts
Temperature Scales:
  • Celsius (°C): Based on water's freezing (0°C) and boiling (100°C) points
  • Fahrenheit (°F): Based on brine's freezing point (0°F) and human body temperature (96°F)
  • Kelvin (K): Absolute temperature scale starting from absolute zero
Interesting Facts:
  • Absolute zero (-273.15°C) is the coldest possible temperature
  • The Sun's surface temperature is about 5,500°C (9,932°F)
  • Human body temperature is typically 37°C (98.6°F)
  • Water boils at different temperatures depending on altitude
