About Age Calculation
How Age is Calculated:

Age calculation takes into account the exact number of years, months, and days between your birth date and the calculation date. It properly handles leap years and varying month lengths.

Leap Years:
  • Years divisible by 4 are leap years
  • Years divisible by 100 are not leap years (unless also divisible by 400)
  • Leap years have 366 days instead of 365
