About Weight Units
Metric System:
  • Milligram (mg): 1/1000 of a gram
  • Gram (g): Base unit of weight
  • Kilogram (kg): 1000 grams
  • Metric Ton (t): 1000 kilograms
Imperial System:
  • Grain (gr): Smallest unit
  • Dram (dr): 27.344 grains
  • Ounce (oz): 16 drams
  • Pound (lb): 16 ounces
  • Stone (st): 14 pounds
  • Hundredweight (cwt): 100 pounds
  • Ton: 2000 pounds (US) or 2240 pounds (UK)
Specialized Units:
  • Carat: Used for gemstones and pearls
  • Troy Ounce: Used for precious metals
  • Atomic Mass Unit: Used in chemistry
  • Solar Mass: Used in astronomy
Usage by Country:
  • Metric: Most countries worldwide
  • Imperial: United States, Myanmar, Liberia
  • Mixed: United Kingdom, Canada
