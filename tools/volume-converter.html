About Volume Units
Metric System:
  • Milliliter (mL): 1/1000 of a liter
  • Liter (L): Base unit of volume
  • Cubic Meter (m³): 1000 liters
  • Cubic Centimeter (cm³): 1 milliliter
US Customary Units:
  • Teaspoon (tsp): 5 milliliters
  • Tablespoon (tbsp): 3 teaspoons
  • Cup (cup): 16 tablespoons
  • Pint (pt): 2 cups
  • Quart (qt): 2 pints
  • Gallon (gal): 4 quarts
Other Units:
  • Imperial Gallon: Used in UK
  • Cubic Foot: Used in construction
  • Cubic Inch: Used in engineering
  • Barrel: Used for oil and liquids
Usage by Context:
  • Cooking: Cups, tablespoons, teaspoons
  • Beverages: Liters, gallons, fluid ounces
  • Construction: Cubic meters, cubic feet
  • Science: Milliliters, cubic centimeters
  • Industry: Barrels, cubic meters
