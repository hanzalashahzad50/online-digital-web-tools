About QR Codes
What are QR Codes?

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of data including URLs, text, contact information, and more.

Error Correction Levels:
  • Low (L): 7% - Good for clean printing
  • Medium (M): 15% - Standard choice
  • Quartile (Q): 25% - Better for small codes
  • High (H): 30% - Maximum error correction
Common Uses:
  • Website URLs
  • Contact information (vCard)
  • WiFi network credentials
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Plain text messages
Tips:
  • Use high contrast colors for better scanning
  • Larger sizes work better for printing
  • Test your QR code before using it
