About Speed Units
Common Speed Units:
  • Kilometers per Hour (km/h): Most common worldwide
  • Miles per Hour (mph): Used in US and UK
  • Meters per Second (m/s): Scientific unit
  • Knots (kn): Used in aviation and maritime
  • Feet per Second (ft/s): Used in some engineering
Specialized Units:
  • Mach Number: Speed relative to speed of sound
  • Speed of Light: Ultimate speed limit
  • Speed of Sound: Varies with medium and temperature
Usage by Context:
  • Road Transport: km/h or mph
  • Aviation: knots or km/h
  • Maritime: knots
  • Scientific: m/s
  • Space: km/s or fraction of light speed
Speed Categories:
  • Subsonic: Below Mach 1
  • Transonic: Around Mach 1
  • Supersonic: Above Mach 1
  • Hypersonic: Above Mach 5
