About Image to PNG Converter

This free online tool allows you to convert images from various formats to PNG format. PNG (Portable Network Graphics) is a lossless image format that supports transparency and is widely used on the web.

How to use:
  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking "Browse Files"
  2. Adjust conversion options if needed (quality, resize)
  3. Click "Convert to PNG" button
  4. Download your converted PNG image
Features:
  • Supports multiple input formats (JPG, GIF, BMP, WEBP, TIFF, etc.)
  • Adjustable output quality
  • Option to resize images
  • Preserves transparency
  • Fast and secure conversion (all processing happens in your browser)
