Scientific Calculator Guide
Basic Operations:
  • Addition: +
  • Subtraction: -
  • Multiplication: * or ×
  • Division: / or ÷
  • Power: ^
  • Percentage: %
Trigonometric Functions:
  • Sine: sin(x)
  • Cosine: cos(x)
  • Tangent: tan(x)
  • Inverse Sine: asin(x)
  • Inverse Cosine: acos(x)
  • Inverse Tangent: atan(x)
Advanced Functions:
  • Square Root: sqrt(x)
  • Natural Logarithm: ln(x)
  • Common Logarithm: log(x)
  • Exponential: exp(x)
  • Factorial: n!
  • Absolute Value: |x|
Rounding Functions:
  • Floor: ⌊x⌋ (round down)
  • Ceiling: ⌈x⌉ (round up)
  • Round: round(x)
