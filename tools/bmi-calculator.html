About BMI
What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. It's a screening tool that may indicate whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Limitations:
  • Doesn't measure body fat directly
  • May not be accurate for athletes
  • Doesn't account for muscle mass
  • May vary by age and ethnicity
Health Implications:
  • Underweight: May indicate malnutrition
  • Normal: Generally healthy weight
  • Overweight: Increased health risks
  • Obese: Higher risk of chronic diseases
Important Note:

BMI is just one indicator of health. Always consult with healthcare professionals for personalized advice.
